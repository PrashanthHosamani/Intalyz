Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Intalyz.io ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your data when you use our OSINT Due Diligence application and services.

2. Information We Collect

When you use Intalyz.io, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Data: Email addresses and profile information when you register or sign in via third-party providers (e.g., Google OAuth).
  • Investigation Data: Entity names, domains, and parameters you input to generate OSINT reports.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform (IP addresses, browser types, timestamp logs).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our OSINT intelligence services.
  • Process your investigation requests and generate PDF reports.
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent abusive activities on our platform.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use third-party APIs (e.g., AlienVault OTX, GitHub API, Google Custom Search) to fulfill your OSINT requests. Your target entity queries are sent to these providers securely.
  • Hosting: Our infrastructure relies on secure third-party hosting (e.g., Render.com).
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, subpoena, or other legal processes.

5. Data Security

We implement robust, industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no electronic transmission over the internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure, so we cannot promise or guarantee that hackers, cybercriminals, or other unauthorized third parties will not be able to defeat our security.

6. Google OAuth Compliance

Intalyz.io uses Google's Application Programming Interface (API) Services to authenticate users. Our use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us via our official support channels.
